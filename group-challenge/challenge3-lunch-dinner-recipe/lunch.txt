Lunch Recipe:⁠
⁠
1. Whisk together 6 large eggs and 2 Tbsp half-and-half.⁠
⁠
2. Stir-fry butter, yellow onion, red bell pepper, jalapeño and the egg mixture.
⁠
3. Combine 1 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese and 1 cup grated Monterey Jack cheese.⁠
⁠
4. Fill the quesadillas with prepared ingredients. 
⁠
5. Cook and seal the quesadillas.
